# Vision | OpenHarness

> "Let AI work tirelessly for you 24/7."

## Our Belief

In the era of AI, we do not lack smart models; what we lack are **systems that can actually get things done**.

Human attention is fleeting. We need to sleep, eat, and rest. AI does not. However, without a proper framework to constrain and guide it, AI easily loses its way in long-term tasks: forgetting where it left off, falling into infinite loops, or hallucinating and prematurely declaring a task complete.

**OpenHarness** was born to solve this exact problem.

We believe that the AI agents of the future should not just be "chatbots", but **tireless digital workers**. They should be as precise as gears and as diligent as workers, operating on predefined tracks day after day, moving data, generating content, and monitoring systems for humans.

## Harness Engineering

The core foundation of OpenHarness is **Harness Engineering**. We do not pursue flashy "self-evolution" or "emergent intelligence"; we pursue **absolute reliability**.

We break down long-term tasks into six mechanical load-bearing components:
1. **Mission**: Writing down "what is considered done" in black and white, rejecting subjective judgment.
2. **Playbook**: Making tacit knowledge explicit, turning it into machine-readable steps.
3. **Tools**: Giving the AI senses and limbs.
4. **Heartbeat**: Using the simplest text files to solve the most complex long-term amnesia.
5. **Eval**: External validation loops, never letting the executor be its own referee.
6. **Cleanup**: Mechanically cleaning up garbage to prevent system collapse.

## The Role of OpenHarness

In the agent ecosystem, if Skills give the model various "superpowers", then OpenHarness puts a **sturdy collar and plow** on these superpowers, turning a wild horse into a reliable ox that can plow the fields day and night.
